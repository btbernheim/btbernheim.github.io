What Are Your Goals?

Setting goals are the single most important step in your fitness regimen. Without them, you would have no direction or way to ensure your progression. Let's say for example, that your goal is to gain 5 lbs. What would you need to do to achieve this goal?

-The Basics: Resistance training regimens are comprised of four main tools:

  • Dumbells
  • Barbells
  • Resistance Machines
  • Body Weight Movements

    • Many novice weight lifters jump right into heavy lifting once they enter the gym

  1. Warm your body up with dynamic stretching
  2. Begin your weight training with a compound movement
  3. Cooldown with some light cardio and stretching

Push Day Routine:

Movement/Lift Set Range Rep Range Rest Time
Incline Bench Press 5 5 3-5 min
Dumbell Press 4 8-12 1.5 min
Dips (weighted) 3 10 1 min
Cable Flys 4 12-15 1 min
Finish up with 15 min of steady state cardio

Pull Day Routine:

Movement/Lift Set Range Rep Range Rest Time
Pull-ups (weighted) 5 5 3-5 min
Bent Over Row 3 8-12 1.5 min
Seated Cable Row 3 10 1 min
Cable Pull-overs 4 12-15 1 min
Finish up with 15 min of steady state cardio

Leg Day Routine:
